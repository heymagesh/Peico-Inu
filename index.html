About Fractal Pengu

Fractal Pengu is a community driven meme coin built on the Solana blockchain. Inspired by fractal mathematics and internet culture, the project celebrates creativity, humor and decentralized community power. The penguin represents resilience, growth and the unpredictable beauty of decentralized ecosystems.
