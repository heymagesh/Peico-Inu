About Fractal Pengu

Fractal Pengu is a community driven meme coin built on the Solana blockchain. Inspired by fractal mathematics and internet culture, the project celebrates creativity, humor and decentralized community power. The penguin represents resilience, growth and the unpredictable beauty of decentralized ecosystems. Just like fractal patterns expand infinitely, the community behind Fractal Pengu continues to grow and evolve. Every holder becomes part of a collective story where memes, culture and blockchain technology merge to create something unique.

Tokenomics

The tokenomics of Fractal Pengu are designed to support long term community participation and ecosystem growth. The project has a total supply of 300 million tokens with 270 million currently circulating in the market and 30 million allocated for vesting. Fractal Pengu is listed across decentralized exchanges including Orca and other Solana DEX platforms.
